BBQ Recipe



Description

This is a simple and easy recipe to make delicious chicken bbq.

We recommend using a charcoal grill for best results.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Rosemary herb
  • Honey

Steps:

  1. Add the BBQ sauce to a bowl.
  2. Add rosemary herb to the sauce.
  3. Sprinkle some salt and pepper to the bowl mixture as required.
  4. Add a hint of honey to the mixture.
  5. Marinate the chicken into the mixture and leave overnight.
  6. Preheat the grill for 20 mins then add the chicken.
  7. Cook for 30-40 mins flipping regularly. Using a kitchen brush, apply honey as desired.
  8. Remove the chicken once fully cooked and let stand for 5 mins before serving.
