Kyiv Chicken Recipe



Description

Delicious Kyiv Chicken recipe a.k.a 'Cotelette de poulet'.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Garlic butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Breading
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Slice the chicken breast into thin layers.
  2. Mix salt and pepper as desired.
  3. Roll up a chunk of garlic butter into the slice of chicken breast.
  4. Add some oil to the outer layer and dip into the breading.
  5. Repeat the step for multiple pieces.
  6. Place onto a non-stick oven platter and back for 20 mins.
  7. Let it stand for 5 mins and serve with desired side.
