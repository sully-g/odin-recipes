Cheeseburger Recipe



Description

This is an easy-to-make cheeseburger recipe that can take only 20 mins to complete.

Ingredients

  • Beef Patty
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese slice
  • Desired Sauces
  • Onions

Steps:

  1. Preheat your pan and add the beef patty at high heat.
  2. Flip the patty regularly for 10 mins.
  3. Add onions to the pan.
  4. Add a slice of cheese to the patty until lightly melted.
  5. Toast the burger buns.
  6. Place the lettuce and sliced tomatoes on the buns.
  7. Add the patty and firmly press the buns together after adding desired sauces.
